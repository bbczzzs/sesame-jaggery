About Us

Our Story

Food made with love, served with warmth

Who We Are

Sesame & Jaggery is a home-based food venture born from a simple belief: the best food comes from home kitchens, made with patience, love, and the finest ingredients.

We are a family passionate about preserving traditional recipes while bringing them to your table with the same care and attention that has been passed down through generations.

Our Philosophy

In a world of mass-produced food, we choose to slow down. Every jar of pickle, every batch of snacks, every sweet treat is handcrafted in small batches. We believe food should nourish not just the body, but also the soul.

Honest

No preservatives, no shortcuts, no compromises

Fresh

Made to order, never sitting on shelves

Homemade

Crafted with the same love as family meals

What We Offer

  • Achar (Pickles)

    Traditional pickles made with family recipes, sun-ripened and aged to perfection

  • Namkeen (Snacks)

    Handmade savoury snacks prepared fresh every week

  • Mithai (Sweets)

    Pure ghee sweets made with natural sweeteners for special occasions

  • Thali (Meals)

    Weekend home-style meals that taste like they came from your mother's kitchen

Our Promise

Every product that leaves our kitchen carries our promise of quality. We use only fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. We never use artificial preservatives or colours. What you get is pure, honest, homemade food — the way it should be.

Location

We operate from our home kitchen in Virar, Mumbai, India. We currently serve customers within Virar and Mumbai areas.
